Clickable Slot Location Selection Mockup

How the manual location selection / relation creation process could look like, for Modules (child) within a DU (parent).

Modeled after the /addpartstree page of the official ProdDB, you can try various (front) DUs here and select the respective modules by clicking them.

Each SU is oriented in such a way that when looking at its face, the module connectors are at the top (or on the right), and module capacitors are on the bottom (or on the left).
